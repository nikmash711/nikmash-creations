DownTheBlock

DownTheBlock gives you a convenient way to stay informed by connecting you with all your neighbors. Lost a pet and desperately need your neighbors help to find him/her? Hear a helicopter hovering above your house and don't know why? Join DownTheBlock today and you'll be up-to-date on everything happening close to home as well as in the broader community. From criminal activity to local events, you'll hear it all from here!

Jewish Blessings

There's a custom in the Jewish culture to say a blessing, or bracha, before and after eating, lighting candles for holidays, and various other actions. I wanted to help make this religious observance more accessible to my Jewish community, so I developed an Alexa skill that enables users to ask Alexa for Jewish blessings and after-blessings on foods, drinks, holidays, and different occasions. The skill was built using Python and AWS, and was published to the Amazon Alexa Skills Store.

Live on the Amazon Alexa Skills Store
Tech Stack:
  • Python
  • AWS
