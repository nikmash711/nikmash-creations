about nikkie

I graduated Summa Cum Laude with a degree in Cognitive Science and a Specialization in Computing from UCLA. During my undergrad years, I immediately fell in love with web development, and I have been designing web apps ever since. I'm currently enrolled in Thinkful's Engineering Immersion Program and I'm on my way to becoming a full-stack developer!

I'm obsessed with details, and for me, web apps are all about the little details. I don't give up until everything looks and behaves exactly the way it's supposed to. So any look or idea you have in mind, whether it's for a business, blog, or event, I guarentee I'll bring to life! And if you don't have a design in mind? No worries - that's my favorite part :)
