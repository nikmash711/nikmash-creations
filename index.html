about nikkie

I graduated Summa Cum Laude from UCLA with a degree in Cognitive Science and a Specialization in Computing. During my undergraduate years, I immediately fell in love with full-stack development; it allowed me to channel my creative energy into developing unique products from scratch, while also enabling me to integrate what I learned about human behavior towards designing a more positive, and accessibility-friendly, user experience.

I'm obsessed with details, and for me, developing apps are all about the little details; I don't give up until everything looks and behaves exactly the way it's supposed to. I’m constantly learning new frameworks and cutting-edge technologies, as well as developing useful and creative products. I strive to learn from those around me — peers, mentors, and co-workers alike — and thus always encourage feedback on my code.

When I’m not coding, I can be found eating ice cream, going to Zumba, or trying to figure out where my cat is hiding!

Creations

Pawfiles

Wouldn't it be nice to keep a timeline of your pet's life? From a list of their vets, vaccination schedule, and medical history, to remembering something hilarious they did the other day, it can be easy to forget important things about your pet. Fur-tunately Pawfiles is here to help you keep track of your pet's most memorable moments! Whether it's a medical record or a cute memory you just don't want to forget, this is the purr-fect place to document it all!
